# Code of conduct

GitNodes contributors and maintainers are expected to make participation
welcoming, respectful, and free from harassment.

Examples of acceptable behavior include constructive technical disagreement,
clear feedback, respect for differing experience levels, and taking
responsibility for mistakes. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment,
discrimination, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption,
and publishing another person's private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful. Reports should be made
privately through the contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); reports will
be reviewed fairly and kept confidential where possible.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
