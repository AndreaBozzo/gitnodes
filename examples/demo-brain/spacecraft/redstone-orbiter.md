Redstone is a solar-powered mapping orbiter, the first flight of the common
Meridian bus. It circles [Mars](../bodies/mars.md) in a low, sun-synchronous
orbit so its [spectro-imager](../instruments/spectro-imager.md) always views the
surface under the same lighting — essential for comparing mineral maps across
seasons. Being close to the Sun, it needs no nuclear power source.
