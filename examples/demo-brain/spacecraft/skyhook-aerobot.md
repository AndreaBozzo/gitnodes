Skyhook is an aerobot — a robotic rotorcraft small enough to fly in
[Titan](../bodies/titan.md)'s thick, low-gravity air, where flight takes a
fraction of the power it would on Earth. It hops between sites, recharging between
flights from [radioisotope power](../concepts/radioisotope-power.md), and sniffs
the atmosphere at each stop with its [sounder](../instruments/atmos-probe.md).
