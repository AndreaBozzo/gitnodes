The Tidewater probe is a radiation-hardened flyby spacecraft built to survive
[Jupiter](../bodies/jupiter.md)'s brutal magnetic environment. Rather than orbit
[Europa](../bodies/europa.md) — which the radiation would not allow for long — it
makes dozens of fast, close passes, sounding the ice with [radar](../instruments/ice-radar.md)
and flying its [plume sampler](../instruments/plume-sampler.md) through any vapour
it can catch. It runs on [radioisotope power](../concepts/radioisotope-power.md).
