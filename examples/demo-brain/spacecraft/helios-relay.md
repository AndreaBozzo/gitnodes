Helios is the program's shared communications relay: a long-lived spacecraft that
parks in the outer system and forwards data from every mission too small to reach
home on its own. It carries no science instruments — its payload is a large
high-gain antenna and a [radioisotope](../concepts/radioisotope-power.md) power
source sized to transmit for fifteen years. It flew out aboard the
[Lodestar](../missions/lodestar.md) mission.
