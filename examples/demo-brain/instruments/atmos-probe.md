The atmospheric sounder samples the gases [Skyhook](../missions/skyhook.md) flies
through, measuring the abundance of methane, ethane, and trace organics at every
altitude. Its profiles traced the rise and fall of methane that revealed
[Titan](../bodies/titan.md)'s active weather cycle.
