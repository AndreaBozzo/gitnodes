When [Europa](../bodies/europa.md) vents fine plumes of water vapour through
cracks in its ice, the plume mass spectrometer flies through them and weighs the
molecules it catches — salts, organics, and dissolved gases carried straight up
from the [ocean](../discoveries/europa-subsurface-ocean.md) below, no drilling
required.
