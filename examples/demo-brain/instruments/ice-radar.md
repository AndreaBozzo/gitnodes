The ice-penetrating radar sends low-frequency pulses through [Europa](../bodies/europa.md)'s
ice shell and times the echoes that bounce back from layers and, crucially, from
the boundary where ice meets liquid water. It is the instrument that turned the
moon's [subsurface ocean](../discoveries/europa-subsurface-ocean.md) from
inference into measurement.
