Saturn is the program's farthest regular destination, a ringed gas giant whose
broad system of icy moons makes it a layered target for exploration. Its largest
moon, [Titan](titan.md), is a world in its own right with a dense atmosphere and
weather.

Reaching Saturn economically depends on a [gravity assist](../concepts/gravity-assist.md)
at Jupiter earlier in the cruise.
