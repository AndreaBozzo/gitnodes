Titan is the only moon with a substantial atmosphere — a thick, hazy nitrogen
sky four times denser at the surface than Earth's. Beneath it lie lakes and seas
of liquid methane and ethane fed by a genuine weather cycle of clouds, rain, and
rivers. That thick, low-gravity air makes Titan the easiest place in the solar
system to fly.

The [Skyhook](../missions/skyhook.md) mission exploits exactly that, cruising the
haze as a robotic aerial explorer.
