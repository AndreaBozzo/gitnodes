Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and the gravitational anchor of
its own miniature system of dozens of moons. Its immense mass makes it the
program's preferred slingshot: a close pass bends a spacecraft's trajectory and
adds speed for free through a [gravity assist](../concepts/gravity-assist.md).

The same gravity flexes its inner moons relentlessly, driving the
[tidal heating](../concepts/tidal-heating.md) that keeps [Europa](europa.md)'s
hidden ocean liquid.
