Europa is the smoothest body in the solar system: a shell of water ice cracked
into reddish ridges, wrapped around a global salt-water ocean that holds more
liquid water than all of Earth's. The ocean stays liquid because
[tidal heating](../concepts/tidal-heating.md) from [Jupiter](jupiter.md) kneads
the moon's interior.

It is the program's highest-priority habitability target, surveyed up close by
the [Tidewater](../missions/tidewater.md) mission.
