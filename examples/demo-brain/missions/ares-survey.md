Ares Survey is Meridian's opening flagship: a global mineral-mapping campaign of
[Mars](../bodies/mars.md) from orbit. Led by [Dr. Elena Vasquez](../people/elena-vasquez.md),
it answers a deceptively simple question — *where, and for how long, was Mars
wet?* — by mapping water-altered minerals at scales no previous orbiter could
resolve. Its findings set the landing sites future surface missions will target.
