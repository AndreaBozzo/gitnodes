Skyhook is an aerial mission to [Titan](../bodies/titan.md): instead of one fixed
lander, it flies a rotorcraft from site to site to sample a world with genuine
weather. Led by [Dr. Mira Tanaka](../people/mira-tanaka.md), it maps how methane
moves through Titan's atmosphere, lakes, and dunes — chemistry that on the early
Earth was a precursor to life. It is currently in build for a late-2030s launch.
