Tidewater is the program's habitability flagship: a campaign of close
[Europa](../bodies/europa.md) flybys to confirm and characterise the moon's
ocean. Led by [Dr. Kwame Osei](../people/kwame-osei.md), it pairs ice-penetrating
radar with a plume sampler so it can both *measure* the ice–water boundary and
*taste* the ocean's chemistry where vapour escapes — the strongest case yet for
liquid water held warm by [tidal heating](../concepts/tidal-heating.md).
