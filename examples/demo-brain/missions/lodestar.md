Lodestar was the program's quiet workhorse — the first flight out, carrying the
[Helios Relay](../spacecraft/helios-relay.md) to its station in the outer system.
On the way it used a [gravity assist](../concepts/gravity-assist.md) at
[Jupiter](../bodies/jupiter.md), and grabbed a campaign of flyby imaging of the
Jovian moons that scoped targets for the missions following behind it.
