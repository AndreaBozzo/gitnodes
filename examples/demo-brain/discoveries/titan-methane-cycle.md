[Skyhook](../missions/skyhook.md)'s atmospheric sounder traced methane rising
after [Titan](../bodies/titan.md)'s rare rainstorms and thinning over the dry
poles — direct evidence of an active "methane cycle" that plays the role water
does on Earth, complete with clouds, rain, and rivers. It reframed Titan from a
frozen curiosity into a working weather system.
