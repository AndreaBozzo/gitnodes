Dr. Elena Vasquez is a planetary geologist and the principal investigator for the
[Ares Survey](../missions/ares-survey.md) mission. Her work on ancient Martian
lake beds shaped the program's early focus on following the water, and she chairs
the science board that ranks targets across the whole [Meridian Program](../programs/meridian-program.md).
