Dr. Mira Tanaka is the lead instrument scientist at [Deepfield Labs](../orgs/deepfield-labs.md),
responsible for calibrating the spectrometers and radar that fly across the
fleet. She leads the [Skyhook](../missions/skyhook.md) atmospheric science team
and is the person operators call when a detector starts drifting mid-mission.
