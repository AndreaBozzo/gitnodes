Dr. Kwame Osei is an oceanographer-turned-planetary-scientist who leads the
[Tidewater](../missions/tidewater.md) mission to [Europa](../bodies/europa.md).
He argued for years that the same physics governing Earth's sea ice could be read
remotely from orbit — the premise the mission's [ice-penetrating radar](../instruments/ice-radar.md)
was built to test.
