The Aurora Agency is the public space agency that charters and operates the
[Meridian Program](../programs/meridian-program.md). It sets science priorities,
runs mission operations and the deep-space network, and owns the open data
archive — but it builds almost no hardware itself, contracting spacecraft and
instruments to industry and university partners.
